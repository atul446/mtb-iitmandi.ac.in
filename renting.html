RENTING GUIDELINES

From the novice to the foremost advanced rider, everyone can have the two-wheel fun for twenty-four hours and explore some native trails.

We at MTB Club aim to provide you the thrill of experiencing riding in the mountains. To fulfil this aim, apart from regular rides organised by the club, you can rent the bikes at nominal rates. You must fill the form, and deposit a refundable security.


Please read given guidelines and rules carefully before taking bike on rent

  1. Always wear face masks at all public places and maintain social distancing at all times.

  2. Always use hand sanitizer before and after the rides.

  3. Bikes will be available for the students/employees to rent.

  4. Students can avail the bikes free of cost on rides organised by the club.

  5. As of now, the following bikes are available for rent:
    • Two, 1×6 geared: Firefox Tempest 6s
    • Three, 1x7 geared: Machcity iBike 7
    • Five, 3×8 geared: Montra Rock 3.1 2018

  6. The Montra bikes will be rented only to the experienced riders and will be decided by the MTB club coordinators.

  7. How to Rent a MTB club’s bike?

    Below are the steps to rent a bike. Please strictly adhere to them.
    1. Fill the form at this link at least 24 hours before the start time of the rental. Only filling the google form does not confirm your booking. However, it is mandatory to first do the online booking. As booking time can be used to give priority on a first come first serve basis.
    2. Collect : During a specific day/time the club coordinators will contact...
    3. Payment :

      Securty Money : At the time of acceptance of the bike, a security money has to be deposited to the official account. The rent will be deducted from this amount and the rest will be refunded after the ride.

      In case the renter finds out that there has been damage to the bike, a corresponding amount will be deducted from the security deposit to cover the damages (in addition to the rent). In case the rental amount (and damages repair if any) is more than the security deposit, the borrower has to pay the remaining amount to the account. The details of the account are as follows :

      UPI Id : thakurlovi@okaxis



    4. Return: The bike needs to be handed over to the lender only and not any third party or to be left stranded at a place. The borrower will be responsible for the bike till it is handed back to the lender. The lender will have a careful look at the condition of the bike to look for damages. The borrower is expected to honestly tell of any damage/issues in the bike to ensure a smooth and safe ride for others.

  8. The following rules need to be strictly followed, failing which the borrower will be banned from all club activities and a strict action may be taken by the institute.
    1. You should not litter, participate or initiate any other activity which may be harmful to the sensitive ecosystem of the route.
    2. Any type of drinking, smoking & using drugs are strictly prohibited during the ride.
    3. If the bikes are used for any unethical and illegal purposes such as transportation of illegal substances etc.
    4. Helmet provided by the club must be worn by the rider at all times.
    6. The lenders should not allow anyone else to ride the bikes lent to them.

    If found violating the above rules, you will be banned from the club activities and a strict disciplinary action might be taken against you.

  9. At the time of receiving the bike, you will be required to sign an undertaking stating above rules and guidelines. The understanding will also state that the MTB Club and the institute will not be liable for any damage or loss of wealth, property or life caused by you.
